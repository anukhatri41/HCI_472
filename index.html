Viola Design System

About

This site provides a visualization and documentation for the design system I created for my portfolio. The branding is meant to be friendly, colorful, and clean. The name has two meanings where viola is the scientific name for violet, referring to the purple theme, and the other meaning comes from my favorite movie character of Viola from She's the Man.
