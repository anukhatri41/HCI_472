Logo Variations

Brandmark

The primary brandmark should be used in cases where space is small, such as social media, or business cards.

Lockup

The lockup should be used for most cases, including a horizontal navigation bar.

Secondary

The secondary logo should be used in cases where there is more room for branding, such as the footer.

Favicon

The favicon logo should be used as a favicon shown next to the page title in the browser tab.

Vertical Lockup

The vertical lockup should be used for a side navigation bar.
